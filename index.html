Composition

Basalt forms the dense outer layers from lava flows.

Obsidian, a volcanic glass, appears near vents.

Volcanic ash settles in soft, layered deposits.

Pumice is light and full of air pockets, formed in explosive eruptions.

Each layer marks a different volcanic phase.
